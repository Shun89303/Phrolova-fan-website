The Overseer Of Fractsidus

A conductor who walks the fine line between life and death

Tragic Origin

A life filled with unexpected suffering, grief, and betrayal led this former musician to align with the Fractsidus. She seeks to rekindle a past from which she was the sole survivor.

Symphony of Souls

Her forte can manipulate and transfigure the frequencies of humans, Echoes, and Tacet Discords alike. A silent wave of her baton attunes the very frequencies of being.

Endless Melancholy

The silent and somber Overseer carries an endless sadness that envelops everyone around her. She weaves music that bridges the gap between life and death.

A New Path

After completing her final task, she made a promise with the leader and left the organization to find her own path. She can now be found in the Lost Bevond.
